=head1 NAME

chicken-ipsum - Generate paragraphs of random chicken noises

=head1 SYNOPSIS

B<chicken-ipsum> [I<NUM>]

I<NUM> is the number of paragraphs to generate. Defaults to 1.

=head1 OPTIONS

=over 4

=item --help

Show a help message and exit.

=item --version

Show version information and exit.

=back
