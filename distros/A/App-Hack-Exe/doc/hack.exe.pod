=head1 NAME

hack.exe - An animated terminal app that pretends to hack a website, just like in the movies

=head1 SYNOPSIS

B<hack.exe> I<HOSTNAME>

I<HOSTNAME> is the host to simulate a hack on.

=head1 OPTIONS

=over 4

=item --help

Show a help message and exit.

=item --version

Show version information and exit.

=back
